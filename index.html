Our School

Welcome to our website and thank you for taking the time to view our school.

Jogjakarta Community School (JCS) was founded in 2012 by Yayasan Suluh Semesta to deliver a quality education programme for children aged three to sixteen. JCS is a very special school with its own unique atmosphere. We offer a curriculum that is internationally recognised and we demand high standards of behaviour and academic success from our children. More importantly, we want learning to be enjoyable, experiential and something our children want to be part of.

JCS delivers a holistic education that is integrated with a modern internationally recognised curriculum. Our students leave JCS not only with internationally compatible academic skills but also with the social, emotional and physical skills that prepare them for the world beyond our school walls. We are supported in every way by a caring community of staff and families.

We want our children to enjoy their time at JCS and to leave with a range of skills that will support them in their chosen pathways. Furthermore, we want our children to take with them happy memories of personal achievements and global friendships. JCS is an excellent choice for your child to learn and we look forward to welcoming you and your family in the near future.
